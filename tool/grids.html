Instructions:

  1. Below, click on the grid image you want to download. This should download a copy of the grid to your "Downloads" folder. We recommend starting with the main grid, but feel free to try the alternate grids too.
  2. Print the grid image you just downloaded to a letter-sized paper, and create your sketch on it. You can use anything! Crayons, color pencils, and markers are a great place to start.
  3. Take a photo of your sketch with your mobile device, trying to keep the phone parallel to the paper.
  4. Crop the image on your mobile device to the bounding box of the grid.
  5. Go to www.panoform.com and upload your image file with the uploader to view it in 360'!
  6. (optional) If on a mobile device, tap on the cardboard icon on the bottom right, then insert your device into a VR viewer like Google cardboard and look around your sketch!
